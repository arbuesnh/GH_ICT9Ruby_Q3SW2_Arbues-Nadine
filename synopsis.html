JOKER
SYNOPSIS
The Joker is a 2019 film about Arthur Fleck, a failed stand-up comedian who descends into madness and turns to a life of crime as the iconic supervillain, the Joker. Set in early 1980s Gotham, the film portrays Arthur's transformation into the Joker and his role in the city's descent into chaos.

THE DARK KNIGHT
SYNOPSIS
The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan. The film follows Batman as he faces off against the Joker, who sets out to create chaos and destruction in Gotham City. As the conflict between Batman and the Joker intensifies, the line between hero and villain becomes blurred. The film explores themes of morality and the consequences of vigilantism, and features stunning action sequences and a standout performance by Heath Ledger as the Joker.

BLACK ADAM
SYNOPSIS
Black Adam is a superhero film starring Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson as the titular character. The movie follows the story of Black Adam, a powerful anti-hero with magical abilities, who seeks revenge against those who have wronged him. As he clashes with the Justice Society of America, a team of superheroes, Black Adam's story explores complex themes of power, morality, and the nature of heroism.

MATRIX
SYNOPSIS
The Matrix is a 1999 science-fiction film directed by the Wachowski Brothers (now known as the Wachowski Sisters). The movie follows the story of Thomas Anderson, a computer programmer and hacker who goes by the name "Neo." After being approached by a group of rebels, Neo learns that his reality is actually a computer-generated simulation created by machines to enslave humanity.

I AM LEGEND
SYNOPSIS
"I Am Legend" is a 2007 sci-fi movie starring Will Smith, based on a novel by Richard Matheson. It follows the story of Dr. Robert Neville, the last surviving human in New York City after a virus turned most of humanity into vampire-like creatures. Neville spends his days searching for a cure while defending himself against the infected. He encounters other survivors but discovers that the infected have become smarter, posing an even greater threat. The film explores themes of isolation, survival, and the human condition, and features intense action sequences and stunning visuals.
